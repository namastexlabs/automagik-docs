**AI That Works While You Sleep**

Cron-based workflow scheduler turning reactive agents into proactive workers. 24/7 autonomous execution with monitoring.

### Key Features

- **Cron Scheduling**: Familiar and powerful time-based automation
- **Proactive Agents**: Transform reactive AI into autonomous workers
- **24/7 Execution**: Continuous operation without supervision
- **Built-in Monitoring**: Track execution and performance metrics
- **Workflow Orchestration**: Chain complex multi-step processes
